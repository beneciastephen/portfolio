EcoQuest

High Fidelity Children's Environmental Conservation Learning App Prototype

EcoQuest is an AR-based educational game designed to teach environmental stewardship through interactive, adventure-driven tasks. Players explore their surroundings using their device’s camera to complete missions like collecting litter or protecting wildlife, all while following a storyline that mirrors a real ecosystem. The app’s intuitive interface, gamified goals, and animated AR elements make learning engaging for users ages 10–16, encouraging real-world eco-friendly behaviors through immersive play.

Tool Used: Figma

The Vanishing

Single page Front-End Website for a Mystery Game

The Vanishing is my first ever web design project, a single-page website created to practice and deepen my understanding of HTML and CSS. This project explores dark, moody aesthetics, intuitive navigation, and interactive elements to enhance the storytelling experience of a mystery-adventure game. By experimenting with typography, layout, and styling techniques, I aimed to create an engaging and immersive design while strengthening my front-end development skills.

Tool Used: Visual Studio Code, Canva AI

Restoring My Crown

Exploring Digital Narratives in Web Design

The main idea of Restoring Your Crown is to explore how media has influenced the self-esteem of Black women by promoting Eurocentric beauty standards. It shares a personal journey of struggling with self-image because of natural hair while educating others on the historical impact media has had on cultural beauty norms. This project is framed as a cultural narrative by combining lived experience with history and research. It highlights how Black hair has been misrepresented in the media, stigmatized in society, and ultimately reclaimed as a symbol of pride and identity. The project is relevant because these harmful standards still affect how Black women see themselves today. It encourages reflection, conversation, and healing.

Tool Used: Canva
