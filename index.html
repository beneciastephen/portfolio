About Me

Aspiring UX/UI Designer

I am a senior at the University of Central Florida majoring in Digital Media on the Web and Interactive Media track, minoring in Computer Science. I'm a proud first-generation American with a passion for designing meaningful digital experiences. As I work toward my degree, I’m pursuing a career in UX/UI design where I can combine creativity, empathy, and problem-solving to make technology feel intuitive and accessible for everyone. I’m also a proud member of Delta Sigma Theta Sorority, Incorporated, which has shaped my commitment to service, leadership, and excellence. Whether I’m prototyping user flows or exploring new design tools, I’m driven by the belief that great design can make a real impact.
