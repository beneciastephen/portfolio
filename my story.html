My Story

Growing up as a first-generation American, I learned early on the value of perseverance and hard work. My parents immigrated to the United States with dreams of providing a better life for our family, and their journey instilled in me a deep appreciation for opportunity and education.

From a young age, I was fascinated by technology and design, often spending hours exploring digital experiences, like drawing and designing in platforms like Procreate and Canva. My father being a tech-nerd like me helped a lot in trying to navigate the industry and figuring out how to use my talents in my future career.

As I pursued my studies in Digital Media at the University of Central Florida, I discovered a passion for UX/UI design. I realized that great design is not just about aesthetics, but about creating meaningful and accessible experiences that resonate with users. This realization has driven me to focus on user-centered design principles, ensuring that my work is both functional and engaging.

While I call this "my" story, it wouldn't be possible without the people who have always supported and believed in me. Even before I could fully understand the significance of their sacrifices, my parents were always there.

So in all, my story is also theirs, as I strive to honor their dreams through my own journey. I thank my parents for teaching me the value of hard work and resilience, shaping me into the woman I am today. As I look to the future, I am excited to continue my journey in UX/UI design, leveraging my skills and experiences to create impactful digital experiences. I am committed to lifelong learning and growth, always striving to push the boundaries of what is possible in design and technology.
